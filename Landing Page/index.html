V-room

Create the perfect classrom environment, anytime, anywhere.

From 1 to 1 million

Want to have a chat with a student? Livestream your lecture to a whole country? V-Room gives you the power to choose how you manage your class, from the number of people allowed to password protected rooms, all the customization tools are at your disposal.

Automatic process

Tired of checking who is missing by counting the number of people online? V-Room lets you create an automatic presence sheet that fills itself and is downloadable. Set delay timers and exceptions and we'll take care of the rest.

Make lectures interactive again

Not only can you record all of your classes for future access, you can take notes, bookmark specific parts and index your whole lecture letting your students know exactly what they are watching.
